How to login

To access your account, please use your registered email and password. If you haven't registered yet, please sign up here. If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team at support@afriwell.com.

How to Book session with our therapists?

Our platform offers a wide range of mental health services, including therapy sessions, counseling, and support groups. We strive to provide a safe and supportive environment where individuals can access the help they need to improve their mental well-being.

How to prepare for your first meeting?

Before your first meeting, take some time to reflect on what you hope to achieve from therapy. Consider jotting down any questions or concerns you'd like to discuss with your therapist. It's also helpful to find a quiet, comfortable space where you can fully engage in the session.

How to use google meet? (Virtual Therapy)

Virtual therapy sessions on our platform are conducted using Google Meet. To join your scheduled session, simply log in to your account at the designated time and click on the provided Google Meet link. Ensure you have a stable internet connection and a quiet, private space for the session.

What is user dashboard

The User Dashboard is your personalized hub on our platform where you can access various features and information related to your mental health journey. From scheduling therapy sessions to tracking your progress, the User Dashboard provides a convenient and centralized space for managing your wellness.
